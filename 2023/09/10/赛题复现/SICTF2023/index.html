$hint_1 = a_1p+b_1q$，$hint_2 = a_2p+b_2q$

则$a_2hint_1 = a_1a_2p+b_1a_2q$，$a_1hint_2 = a_1a_2p + b_2a_1q$

两式相减得$a_2hint_1-a_1hint_2 = (b_1a_2-b_2a_1)q$

于是$q = gcd(n,a_2hint1-a_1hint_2)$，可以做检验
